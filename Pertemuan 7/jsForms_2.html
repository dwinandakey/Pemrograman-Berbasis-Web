Temperature Conversion Formula
Celsius Fahrenheit Kelvin Rankine Réaumur
Celsius - °F = °C × 9/5 + 32 K = °C + 273.15 °R = °C × 9/5 + 491.67 °Ré = °C × 4/5
Fahrenheit °C = (°F - 32) × 5/9 - K = (°F + 459.67) × 5/9 °R = °F + 459.67 °Ré = (°F - 32) × 4/9
Kelvin °C = K - 273.15 °F = K × 9/5 - 459.67 - °R = K × 9/5 °Ré = (K - 273.15) × 4/5
Rankine °C = (°R - 491.67) × 5/9 °F = °R - 459.67 K = °R × 5/9 - °Ré = (°R - 491.67) × 4/9
Réaumur °C = °Ré × 5/4 °F = °Ré × 9/4 + 32 K = °Ré × 5/4 + 273.15 °R = °Ré × 9/4 + 491.67 -
